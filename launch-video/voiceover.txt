Your best coding dataset already exists. It is the work you do every day.

BashGym captures and imports AI coding sessions from Claude Code, Gemini CLI, OpenCode, Codex, and Copilot CLI — tool calls, edits, commands, and outcomes — and turns them into structured traces.

Then it separates signal from noise: six-part quality scoring, verification, segmentation, and PII redaction. Successful traces become supervised training data. Failed attempts can become preference pairs. Gaps can be filled with Data Designer.

From there, train your own open-weight specialist with SFT, DPO, GRPO, RLVR, or distillation — on local, private, or cloud compute.

BashGym can even search for better hyperparameters, trace recipes, and data-generation schemas.

Export adapters, merged weights, or GGUF. Serve locally. Route the right tasks to the student, with teacher fallback when confidence is low.

Code. Verify. Curate. Train. Route. Repeat.

BashGym does not just help you build with AI. It turns the way you build into the system that improves your AI.
